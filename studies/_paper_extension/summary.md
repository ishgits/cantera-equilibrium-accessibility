# Paper extension — fiducial C2H2/HCN = 2.1

Direct comparison to Madan & Pearce (2025): which amino acids are equilibrium-accessible with **no NH3**, and the yield (% of initial HCN) as NH3 rises from 1% to 10% of water.

## Accessible with NO ammonia (2)
alanine, proline

## Unlocked only by NH3 (16)
arginine, asparagine, asparticacid, glutamicacid, glutamine, glycine, histidine, isoleucine, leucine, lysine, phenylalanine, serine, threonine, tryptophan, tyrosine, valine
